About us

PharmaDIA was founded in 2015 by pharmacists from medicine regulatory and distribution fields. From the beginning the company was providing regulatory, pharmacovigilance and distribution service in Baltic countries and working as a wholesaler of high quality medicine, food supplements and cosmetic products. The company is not only wholesaler, but the developer of cosmetic products as well.
